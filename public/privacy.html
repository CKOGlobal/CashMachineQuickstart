We use your information to:

4. SMS Messaging and Consent

Opt-In: By purchasing Cash Machine QuickStart ($67), you explicitly consent to receive SMS text messages from us for accountability check-ins.

Message Frequency: You will receive 3 messages per week (Monday, Wednesday, Friday) for 90 days, totaling approximately 36 messages.

Message Content: Messages include accountability check-ins, milestone reminders, progress questions, and coaching support.

Opt-Out: You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any SMS message. You will receive a confirmation message, and no further messages will be sent.

Help: Reply HELP for assistance, or contact us at Kelli@proactively-lazy.com

Carrier Charges: Message and data rates may apply as per your mobile carrier's plan.

Supported Carriers: Major U.S. carriers are supported. Service availability may vary.
